The Great Falls Race

The festival's most dramatic and dazzling event is the Great Falls Race. This downriver race is a quick, adrenaline-packed descent of 60 vertical feet through the Potomac's spectacular class V+ Great Falls. Watch top kayakers from around the nation dash down the falls and compete for the Great Falls Race trophy. The exact course depends on the water level, but will take place on one of the following:

Maryland Lines

Course starts on river left shore just left of the large rock in the middle of the channel above Pummel. Racers will navigate Pummel and then choose to either go through Pencil Sharpener or Z-turn. Racers will then navigate Horseshoe and finish at a location explained at the competitors meeting.

Virginia Lines

Course starts on the river right shore at the large vertical slab rock. Racers then choose from the three lines on the entrance rapid of the VA lines and then navigate S-bend. Racers complete the course after they have navigated the Spout and reached the furthest downstream point of the flake at a location explained at the competitors meeting.

Center Lines

Course starts on the river right shore above the VA line entrance rapid at the large vertical slab rock. Racers will ferry across the inflow to the VA lines and navigate Grace Under Pressure. Racers then navigate the rapid leading to the middle finger and the middle finger. The finish line is the rocky outcropping on the flake about 100 ft. downstream of the streamers at a location explained at the competitors meeting.

Fishladder

The Fish Ladder race course starts in an eddy/cove upstream of the first slide on river left along the C&O canal towpath. Racers must navigate the first two slides or the sneak in tight quarters channel. Racers then will enter the crux of the run, where they will navigate a pre-determined line: either slides 3 and 4, the Back Canyon, or BiTc# Monkey, TBD at the race meeting. The finish will be below the fifth(last) slide at a location explained at the competitors meeting. The cut off for a fish ladder race is 4.8 feet.
